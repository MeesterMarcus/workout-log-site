FAQ

Questions about Pro

How much does IronStack Pro cost?

IronStack Pro offers three plans: Monthly at $2.99/mo (auto-renews), Yearly at $19.99/yr (saves 44% vs monthly), or a one-time Lifetime upgrade for $39.99. All plans unlock the same Pro features. The Lifetime plan is the best value — pay once and own Pro forever.

Is there a lifetime option?

Yes. The Lifetime plan is a single one-time payment of $39.99. No renewals, no recurring charges — ever. Hevy's lifetime is $74.99 and Strong's is $99.99; IronStack's is less than half the price of either.

What happens if I don't upgrade?

Nothing bad. The free tier works great — no ads, no paywalled nags during workouts, no degraded experience. You can log unlimited workouts, but you're capped at 3 saved templates, 3 AI generation tries, and a text-based plate calculator. When you're ready to go deeper, Pro is there.

Where do I purchase Pro?

Inside the IronStack app — go to Settings and tap Upgrade to Pro. The purchase is processed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android), so it's handled securely by Apple or Google.

Can I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Yes. Use the "Restore Purchase" option in Settings to restore your Pro access on any new device linked to the same App Store or Google Play account.

Is AI Coach available in Spanish?

AI Coach is currently available in English only. All other features — including AI workout generation — are fully available in both English and Spanish.
